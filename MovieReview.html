Interstellar


Rating: 8.6/10

Genre: Sci-Fi, Adventure, Drama

Director: Christopher Nolan

Year: 2014

Duration: 169 minutes

Review

Interstellar is a visually stunning and emotionally powerful sci-fi epic. Christopher Nolan takes us on a journey across galaxies to explore love, sacrifice, and humanity’s survival. Hans Zimmer’s haunting score, breathtaking visuals, and a heartfelt story make it one of the greatest space films ever made. This is not just a movie—it’s an experience.
